About Us

The passion for gems, passed down through generations

Our Story

Officina Gemmologica was born in the heart of Rome's historic center from the shared passion of father and daughter for the fascinating world of precious stones.

What started as a small artisan workshop is today a laboratory of excellence in gem cutting, where tradition and innovation merge to create unique pieces of extraordinary beauty.

With over twenty years of experience in the goldsmith craft and gemology, we put at our clients' disposal expertise gained through study, dedication, and love for our craft.

Our Philosophy

We believe that every stone has a story to tell, a unique character waiting to be revealed. Our approach is that of the craftsman of old: we work each gem entirely by hand, dedicating time and attention to every single detail.

"We don't mass produce. Each of our pieces is unique, characterized by a truly artisan soul."

Our Laboratory

Our laboratory, located in Rome's prestigious historic center, is equipped with precision instruments for cutting and working the most precious gems.

Here we work with stones comprehensively: from sourcing the best rough material, to cutting the gem, to creating the finished jewel. Each phase is executed with the same dedication and attention to detail.
