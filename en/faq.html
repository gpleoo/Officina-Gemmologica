Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services

Services and Work

We work with all types of precious and semi-precious stones: diamonds, rubies, sapphires, emeralds, aquamarines, tourmalines, topazes, amethysts and many others. We handle both natural stones and some types of synthetic stones for specific applications.

Absolutely! We work both with our own stones and with material provided by the client. Before proceeding, we evaluate the stone to recommend the most suitable cut to maximize its beauty and value. We also offer re-cutting services for already worked gems that need corrections.

We perform all classic cuts and many custom fantasy cuts:

  • Faceted cuts: brilliant, princess, emerald, oval, marquise, pear, heart, cushion
  • Cabochon cuts: for opals, turquoise, star stones
  • Fantasy cuts: custom designs on request

Times vary based on the complexity of the work:

  • Standard cut: 3-7 business days
  • Complex/fantasy cut: 1-2 weeks
  • Special projects: 2-4 weeks

We always provide a precise estimate before starting the work.

Appointments and Visits

For personalized consultations, evaluations and quotes, we strongly recommend making an appointment by phone or email. This allows us to dedicate all the necessary time to you.

For general information or to see the laboratory, you can stop by during opening hours, but availability may be limited without advance notice.

  • Monday - Friday: 09:30 - 18:30
  • Saturday: 10:00 - 13:00 (by appointment only)
  • Sunday: Closed

During holidays, hours may vary. We recommend contacting us to confirm.

Our laboratory is located in Rome's historic center, easily accessible by public transport. The exact address and directions are available on our Contact page.

Prices and Payments

Prices depend on several factors:

  • Type of stone and its characteristics
  • Complexity of the requested cut
  • Size of the gem
  • Estimated processing time

We always provide a free, detailed quote before starting any work, with no obligation on your part.

We accept various payment methods:

  • Cash
  • Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Maestro)
  • Bank transfer

For high-value work, it's possible to arrange a customized payment plan with an initial deposit and balance upon delivery.

For most work, we require a 30-50% deposit at the time of order confirmation. The balance is due upon delivery of the completed work. For small jobs, we may not require a deposit.

Guarantees and Support

Yes, all our work is guaranteed. In case of workmanship defects, we provide corrections at no additional cost. The warranty does not cover damage caused by improper use, impacts or accidents.

Yes, all stones entrusted to our laboratory are covered by our professional insurance. Upon delivery, we issue a detailed receipt with description and declared value of the stone.
