1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we process personal data of users who visit the website www.officinagemmologica.com (hereinafter "Website"). This notice is provided pursuant to Article 13 of EU Regulation 2016/679 (GDPR) to those who interact with web services accessible electronically.

2. Data Collected

2.1 Navigation data

The computer systems and software procedures used to operate this website acquire, during their normal operation, certain personal data whose transmission is implicit in the use of Internet communication protocols.

This data includes:

  • IP addresses
  • Browser type used
  • Operating system
  • Domain names and addresses of websites from which access was made
  • Date and time of visit
  • Pages visited

2.2 Voluntarily provided data

The optional, explicit and voluntary sending of messages via the contact form on the Website involves the acquisition of data entered by the user, such as:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Message content

3. Purposes of Processing

Personal data is processed for the following purposes:

  • Responding to requests: managing requests for information, quotes or consultations sent via the contact form
  • Service improvement: anonymous statistical analysis to improve navigation and Website content
  • Legal obligations: fulfilling obligations required by law, regulation or community legislation

4. Legal Basis for Processing

The processing of personal data is based on:

  • Consent: for sending communications via the contact form
  • Legitimate interest: for technical management of the Website and IT security
  • Legal obligation: to comply with legal obligations

5. Data Retention

Personal data will be retained for the time necessary to achieve the purposes for which they are processed and in any case for a period not exceeding 24 months from collection, unless otherwise required by law.

6. Your Rights

Under Articles 15-22 of the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: obtain confirmation of the existence of personal data concerning you
  • Rectification: obtain correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: obtain deletion of data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: obtain restriction of processing
  • Portability: receive data in a structured format
  • Object: object to processing
  • Withdraw consent: withdraw consent at any time

To exercise your rights, you can send a request to: info@officinagemmologica.com
