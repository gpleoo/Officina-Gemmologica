Custom Gem Cutting

Cutting is the art of transforming a rough stone into a gem that captures light and reveals all its beauty. Each cut is designed based on the unique characteristics of the stone to maximize its brilliance and color.

We perform all types of cuts: brilliant, emerald, princess, marquise, oval, pear, cabochon and custom fantasy cuts according to your needs.

  • Custom cutting for each stone
  • All classic and fantasy cut types
  • Re-cutting and correction of existing gems
  • Work with precious and semi-precious stones

Gemological Consulting

We offer professional consultations to help you understand the value and characteristics of your stones. In-depth analysis with professional gemological instruments to identify nature, treatments and quality.

  • Gem identification and analysis
  • Valuation and value estimation
  • Detection of treatments and synthetics
  • Purchase and investment consulting
  • Appraisals for insurance and estates

Jewelry Design

We transform your ideas into unique jewelry. From the first sketch to the finished product, we accompany you through every phase of creation, caring for every detail with artisan passion.

  • Custom design to your specifications
  • 3D rendering of the project
  • Choice of materials (gold, platinum, silver)
  • Professional stone setting
  • Artisan finishing and polishing

Precious Stone Sourcing

Looking for a particular stone? Thanks to our network of suppliers and our experience, we can find rare and high-quality gems from around the world.

  • Commission-based gem search
  • Access to international suppliers
  • Careful selection based on your needs
  • Ethical sourcing guarantee
  • Certification included

Restoration and Repair

Family jewelry deserves a second life. We carefully restore antique and vintage jewelry, preserving their original character while bringing them back to their former glory.

  • Conservative restoration of antique jewelry
  • Repair of damaged settings
  • Replacement of missing stones
  • Ring resizing
  • Professional cleaning and polishing

Training and Workshops

We share our passion and knowledge through courses and workshops dedicated to those who want to approach the world of gemology or deepen their skills.

  • Introduction to gemology
  • Precious stone recognition
  • Practical cutting workshops
  • Customized courses on request
